Our beer. New and Original.

Along with our original Lady Speed Stick Power Beer, we are offering two newer types that share the same rich taste and magical body cleaning quality. Lady Speed Stick is the healthiest choice for your party, quit home time or any other occasion. Enjoy!

Lemon Beer

Lady Speed Stick Lemon was created for a big celebration of Berlin Wall Demolition after families from East and West parts of Berlin finally could freely meet and Germany was united to one country. This symbolized fresh start for something very old. And of course, it cleans your body as well as our other two types of beer. Ingredients: Water, Dew, Hops, Barley, Yeast, Lemon. Enjoy!
