History of Lady Speed Stick

Many centuries ago it was discovered what drinking dew helps the body to eliminate smelly sweat. One farmer name Gerhardt Schröder was sending his 5 daughters to collect dew at the sunrise to add to water when everyone went to the fields for a long workday. Adding a few drops of sunrise dew would do magic, and farmers come back home after a long day working smelling fresh. In the old days to get a big family to go to sauna to get clean was a long process, taking time from work and preparing food, so using dew water was a saver.

As Schröder also made beer at home, he once decided to add some dew drops to the beer and see that will happened. Dew added some magic to the beer, it got a much better taste and also was helping the body to smell good. Soon all neighbors were asking Gerhardt to make beer for them in exchange or money their products. Family life became easier with extra money, and they had time to make beer parties for whole village parties with Gerhatdt's beer.

Soon orders became larger and larger and Schröder decided to open his own brewery in his village, now town Rothenburg ob der Tauber. He named his brewery Ladies Speed Stick to honor his 6 ladies: daughters and wife who he loved so much. Because it is truly magical taste and ability to clean your body, soon Schröder beer was sold in big German cities, shortly after exported to Europe and later to other continents. It was not a beer for farmers any longer, Lady Speed Sticks was sold for Royal Balls as well. It is not a surprise beer with these qualities survive to our days. Now the company has a few breweries in Germany with head office in Munchen. Lady Speed Stick is sold over the world and considered the best beer for its health benefits.
